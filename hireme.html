Who Am I?

Hi, I'm Nikki! I'm a web developer for small businesses, independent contractors, and organizations who want to take their businesses and goals to the next level.

I am passionate about building things that automate the mundane and building beautiful, thoughtful websites that work flawlessly on all devices.

Previously I worked as a park ranger which allowed me to spend quality time outdoors and experience life in different parts of the United States.

The outdoors inspires my work and drives me to build beautiful, thoughtful creations.

My experience as a park ranger and classroom educator gave me extensive experience with organizing and leading teams, public speaking, and being flexible/problem solving when last minute changes arose.

Since 2020 I have worked on web apps and websites using frameworks including React and Angular, as well as HTML, CSS, Javascript.

I have also worked on numerous Shopify sites, ensuring that my clients' customers have flawless shopping experiences on all devices.

In my free time I enjoy going on drives, taking and editing photos, coding my own projects, staying up-to-date with the latest web development trends, and hanging out with friends.
