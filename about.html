Meet Nikki

Nikki is the driving force behind Nikkster.tech. She has been coding professionally since 2020 and unprofessionally since the Myspace days. Since then, she has built and edited sites for multiple E-commerce brands, small businesses, and organizations.

At Nikkster.tech, Nikki takes all the knowledge she's gained from designers, developers, and product managers over the years and brings them directly to you. She combines her passion of helping others and technology by providing clients with modern, sleek websites that not only help her clients reach their goals, but also exceed their expectations.

Originally from Southern California, Nikki spent 5 years working in National Parks across the USA. Before that, she worked with local non-profits on active transportation and public land initiatives and taught ecology and water education in the local schools. Currently she resides in Washington State, where she enjoy the easy access to trees and water.

When she decided to take the leap and make a career switch, Nikki noticed that empathy & communication skills were lacking in the tech world. That's why she strives to:

  • design beautiful and functional sites that acheive her clients' goals
  • create sites that work flawlessly on both desktop and mobile (we live in a mobile age, after all!)
  • be upfront & honest about expectations, milestones, and cost
  • try to reach clients within their budget
  • keep clients in the loop, always
  • build exceptional websites that her clients will love
